Good to Learn Foreign Language


            Language is what makes us human. It is the medium we use to share our though. With the world. Could you imagine thought without language? Great language also has a wonderful musical quality. Learning a new language is like learning a new way to think and a now way to act. Therefore, I have five reasons to agree with the statement which states that it is a good idea for everyone to loam a foreign language."

First of all. We learn foreign language because of school curriculum. School curriculum requires us to learn foreign language. As we can see. The Ministry of Education. Youth and Sport has set foreign Language — English as one of important subject. In school curriculum. That is why all Cambodian students need to study foreign language from elementary school until high school. Thus, it is really good to loam foreign language to follow our school curriculum.

Second. Learning foreign language has important factor in helping us to develop ourselves. For example. In study especially at the university or college, most of the books and document are written in English. Not only the books and documents but also the assignments are written in English. Therefore, if we do not know English, we actually cannot get along with the lesson and find it hard in doing the assignment. As a consequence, we cannot build up our knowledge and strengthen our abilities. That is why we will fail automatically or get lower score from doing assignment or doing the exam. However, if we learn or know foreign language. All the problems mentioned above will be avoided.

The other reason of learning foreign language is advancement or instrumental purpose. It means that learning language is like a stepping stone for us to get a job. It is true since the industry and information revolution, most employers seek for workers who have the ability of foreign language. To take an example, in applying for the job, we need to write curriculum vitae and cover letter in English. In other word, during the interview for the job we always use foreign language as especially English. That is why if we do not have the foreign language ability, we will be the last choice them.

Joining the global community is one of the reasons in learning foreign language. Nowadays, the majority oldie, world speaks English. We am so isolated that we hardly realize this. 'Ile truth is new thoughts and ideas are happening everywhere and they are not being immediately translated. The world is getting smaller and we are coming into contact with the people who use English all the time. If we are businessmen, we can gain advantages for ourselves in business and personal relationship by being able to communicate with people in international language. This will set you apart and gain you immediate respect and credibility if you know foreign language.

Last, we learn foreign language because of specific purpose. Personally, studying abroad is my dream in learning foreign language. In order to pursuit studying abroad, I must have a good knowledge in foreign language. It is because I need to take an exam — the TOEFL or IELTS to reach my point. Despite °flaking exam, I also need to use foreign language in communicating when I have a chance to study abroad. This is the reason that learning foreign language is good for me.

In conclusion, there are live reasons that learning foreign language is good. These reasons include school curriculum, self-development, advancement/ instrument purpose, joining the global community, and specific purpose. Based on these reasons, we can conclude that it is a good idea for everyone to learn foreign language. Therefore. I recommend to all the people especially students please try hard in study either our language or foreign language because what you have learned will benefit you one day.
