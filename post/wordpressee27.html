មេរៀនទី ១១ ៖​ តើអ្វីជាUsers?

Users គឺជា កន្លែង សំរាប់អោយ admin គ្រប់គ្រង account user ផ្សេងទៀត ឬក៏ កំណត់policy ទៅលើ account use​r មានដូចជា: 

·        Subscriber

·       Contributor

·        Author

·        Editor

·        Administrator

Example: Admin change user information, or delete users. 

1.  Subscriber : somebody who can only manage their profile

2.  Contributor : somebody who can write and manage their own posts  but cannot publish them.

3.  Author : somebody who can publish and manage their own posts.

4.  Editor : somebody who can publish and manage posts including the posts of other users.

Administrator : somebody who has access to all the administration features within a single site.
